About this track

This was created for a project in my music production course. The task was to create electronic music with just field recordings that I recorded myself. I used various noises from nature, using rocks/branches for percussive noises. Additionally I took advantage of vehicle noises around campus and my xbox starting noise. Lastly, I used a similar method as Alvin Lucier to transform my voice to adapt to my room's acoustics and create an new noise from this. All together I made this cool synthy houseish beat.
